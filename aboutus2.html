About Me

Theresa McDonald

t.yueji@gmail.com

I am a junior currently attending Rochester Institute of Technology for a BFA in Film and Animation (2D focus) and a minor in Chinese Language.

I am passionate about telling stories through my artwork, whether it's fine art, photography, or motion graphics. I also enjoy helping and communicating with others. As an adoptee from China, I have taken it into my own hands to incorporate that part of my life into my identity by learning the language. In the summer of 2017, I also took intensive Chinese language classes at Peking University in Beijing, while gaining a new perspective on the culture. I am quick to learn and I constantly strive to expand my skill set.


Languages: English (native), Mandarin Chinese (conversational), Spanish (basic/conversational)


2D Animation

I have experience in all aspects of production regarding a 2D animated short film. Pre-production character designs and concept art, character animation, and even post-productino video editing.

Fine Art

I enjoy using other artistic mediums outside of animation like illustration and calligraphy. I've used oils and acrylics, charcoal, watercolor, ink, and more.

Photography

I am a self taught photographer that is actively seeking to improve my abilities and capture the world around me.
